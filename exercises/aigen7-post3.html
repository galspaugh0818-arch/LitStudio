Designing Tools Not Replacements

Published October 2025 • By Gregory Alspaugh

Creators should think of AI as a collaborator. This is a design challenge: craft interfaces that keep the human in control, provide provenance, and surface suggestions without taking authorship away.

Interfaces that inform

Show provenance and suggested edits clearly. Let users accept, modify, or reject AI suggestions so the final artifact remains owned by a human author.
