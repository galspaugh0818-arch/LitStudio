Moderation, Misinformation, and the Attention Economy

Published October 2025 • By Gregory Alspaugh

Platforms are struggling to keep pace with generative misinformation. The economics of attention reward rapid, sensational content, and AI accelerates this loop. Practical moderation strategies must balance speed with human oversight.

Why speed matters

Automated systems can flag content quickly, but false positives and negatives are common. A hybrid model — rapid automated triage followed by targeted human review — reduces harm while scaling effectively.
